MOBILE STRATEGIST / SOLUTIONS ARCHITECT


Location: Cochin


Who are we looking for




Cerebtech is looking to hire a Mobile Strategist / Solutions Architect to help in the delivery of Cerebtech's Mobile Strategy and app development engagements. Typical travel is 30-50% for this position.

The Mobile Strategist / Solutions Architect will work on a variety of exciting project engagements with an exceptional team of professionals. This individual must have assisted organizations in the formulation, planning, and execution of a mobility strategy and delivery of a mobile app. The candidate must be familiar with the current state of mobile technology and mobile best practices throughout a variety of industries. Must be knowledgeable about mobile development standards, have the ability to identify a landscape of mobile vendors (MDM, MADPs, etc.), be familiar with the deployment of mobile apps across platforms, and have created a center of excellence design and execution for mobility. Must have led project teams through a mobile app SDLC.



What will you be doing?


  • Lead, participate in, and execute the delivery of Cerebtech's mobile strategy engagements for client organizations.

  • Lead, participate in, and execute the delivery of Cerebtech's mobile app SDLC for the development of client apps.

  • Understand enabling components: security, infrastructure, mobile device management (MDM), application development environments (MADPs), user experience for mobile, COE, operations support.

  • Collaborate with team members and clients to produce mobile best practices and processes.


Requirement



  • BA/BS from accredited school, MBA preferred.

  • At least 4 years in roles including management consulting and business strategy.

  • At least 2 years of experience with mobile projects.

  • Experience in facilitating strategy/innovation sessions with both business and technology participants.

  • Knowledge of mobile market including key players, emerging technologies, and new business models (including platforms, MDM solutions, network architecture and other mobile technology).

  • General knowledge of enterprise IT architectures, web services, and business processes.
