MOBILE DEVELOPER / LEAD (iOS / ANDROID)


Location: Cochin


Requirement (iOS Lead / Developer)




  • BS/MS in CS, or equivalent. Additional Certifications in the relevant fields preferred.

  • 4+ years of professional development experience. Work experience for a US client would be an added advantage.

  • Minimum of 3 years hands on experience in the development of mobile applications and solutions.

  • Experience with iOS platform (apps for iPhone and iPad) is a MUST.

  • Experience with developing native Applications using Objective-C, Xcode, Cocoa Touch.

  • Should have strong understanding of major iOS Design & Development paradigms spanning User Experience (native, HTML 5/Webkit), data ingestion, Story Boards, Web Analytics, Web Services and REST.

  • Candidate must have independently designed and developed mobile applications.

  • Candidate should be familiar with and articulate the differences and trade-offs between competing mobile platforms such as Android, Windows mobile, RIM, BlackBerry, iPhone, IPad or Symbian.

  • Experience on backend technologies like SQLite, Core Data.

  • Experience with HTML5, JQuery Mobile, PhoneGap or Appcelerator is a plus.

  • Should have excellent communication, client interaction and interpersonal Skills.
  • Any exposure on Swift, Kony, Xamarin will be an additional advantage.

  • Expert knowledge on one of the following Objective-C or Android SDK in Java environment.
